# CropWatch Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Target Cohort:** 10th Cohort - Team 8
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Product Overview

CropWatch is an AI-driven agricultural mobile application. It empowers everyday rural farmers to quickly identify crop diseases by utilizing AI image processing combined with standard language or Nigerian Pidgin symptom descriptions.

### Problem Statement
Rural farmers often face delayed diagnoses because agricultural extension offices can be up to 40 km away. This bottleneck results in:
- Unnecessary crop losses due to rapidly spreading diseases.
- Unaffordable recommendations for imported chemical treatments instead of local remedies.
- Zero tracking of historical farm health problems.
- Language barriers that prevent adoption of existing internet resources.

## 2. Target Users & Use Cases

### User Personas
- **Smallholder Farmer:** Primary target. Needs fast, reliable crop diagnosis with accessible treatments.
- **Commercial Farmer:** Needs tracking functionality for multiple crops across seasons.
- **Agricultural Student:** Uses the application for educational identification.
- **Extension Officer:** Uses the platform as a fast second-opinion tool in the field.

### Key Use Cases
1. **Immediate Diagnosis:** Take a picture, add a symptom note in Pidgin, get a diagnosis with a confidence indicator instantly.
2. **Localized Treatment:** Receive treatment suggestions focusing strictly on accessible organic goods (e.g., neem oil, wood ash, crop rotation) before transitioning to chemicals.
3. **Seasonal Prevention:** Automatically log the diagnosis so the farmer gets pre-plant prevention tips for the next season.

## 3. Product Features (MVP)

- **Photo Upload + Symptom Description:** Both visual and text-based input.
- **AI-Powered Diagnosis:** Top 2-3 most likely problems with Confidence Scores (High, Medium, Low).
- **Severity Indicator:** Color coded: Mild (Green), Moderate (Yellow), Severe (Red - remove plants).
- **Treatment Recommendations:** Locally available options first.
- **Language Toggle:** Instantly switch the entire app between English and Nigerian Pidgin.
- **Crop Support:** Tomato, Cassava, Maize, Yam, Pepper, Rice, Cowpea, Cocoa.
- **History Log & Prevention Tips:** Automatically tracks issues for future season advising.

## 4. Freemium Model

**Free Tier:**
- Up to 5 leaf scans per day.
- Basic basic symptom identification.
- Local/organic solution guidance.
- Last 10 saved diagnoses.
- Offline basic symptom library.

**Paid (Pro) Tier ($10/month):**
- Unlimited leaf scans.
- Full access to industrial chemical databases.
- Unlimited history and cloud backups.
- Full offline AI mode (No Internet required).
- 1-on-1 Chat/Video Call with Extension Officers.
- Exportable PDF Reports.

## 5. Success Metrics

- **Adoption:** Total Active Farmers, Diagnosis Volume, Retention Rate.
- **Trust:** Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT), Referral Rate, App Store rating (highlighting Pidgin usability).
- **Efficacy:** AI Confidence targeting > 85%, Early Detection Ratio (Mild vs Severe), Treatment Cost Savings.
